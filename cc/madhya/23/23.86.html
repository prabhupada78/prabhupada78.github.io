Caitanya-caritamrta: Madhya-lila

23.86

yei gunera ’vasa’ haya krsna bhagavan

SYNONYMS

ananta guna—unlimited qualities; sri-radhikara—of Srimati Radharani; pancisa—twenty-five; pradhana—chief; yei gunera—of those qualities; vasa—under the control; haya—is; krsna—Lord Krsna; bhagavan—the Supreme Personality of Godhead "Similarly, Srimati Radharani has unlimited transcendental qualities, of which twenty-five qualities are principal.

Sri Krsna is controlled by these transcendental qualities of Srimati Radharani.
